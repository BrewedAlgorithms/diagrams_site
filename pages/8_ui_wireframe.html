Understanding the User Journey

Our UI wireframe shows how customers move through the Smart Checkout experience with intuitive, straightforward steps. Here's what makes our interface special:


Inclusive Welcome Screen

The journey begins with a welcoming interface that supports multiple languages and voice assistance, making our system accessible to everyone. Clear instructions guide users to place their items in the scanning zone to begin.

Intelligent Scanning

As customers place items in the scanning area, our system provides real-time feedback showing the number of items detected and scanning progress. The AI-powered product recognition works automatically, with weight verification ensuring accuracy without any manual barcode scanning.

Detailed Cart Review

Customers can review their items with clear pricing displayed for each product. The cart screen also shows the running total and allows users to apply discount codes or see loyalty points earned, creating transparency and trust in the checkout process.

Flexible Payment Options

Our system offers multiple payment methods tailored to local preferences, including secure biometric authentication, mobile/NFC payments supporting popular apps like UPI, PayTM and PhonePe, traditional card payments, and cash options. This flexibility ensures every customer can pay their preferred way.

Always-Available Help

Help is just a tap away at every stage of the checkout journey. Customers can access video tutorials, frequently asked questions, or call for staff assistance whenever needed, ensuring they never feel stuck or confused.

Easy Navigation Controls

The interface features intuitive back buttons allowing customers to correct mistakes or make changes at any point. This creates a forgiving system that accommodates the natural shopping process where decisions may change during checkout.
